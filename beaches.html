Beaches

The authoritative sources, brought together.

RipCo does not replace the official sources for beach conditions. It brings them together in one place, so you can check wind, swell, tide, water temperature, patrol flags and hazard reports without opening six different sites. Each condition below links to its authoritative source.

The authorities

Where the information comes from.

These are the authoritative sources for Australian beach conditions and safety. RipCo brings them together and points you back to them. It does not replace them.

BeachSafe

Surf Life Saving Australia's public guide to beaches, patrol information, flags and hazards.

Surf Life Saving Australia

The national body for surf lifesaving, patrols and beach-safety education.

sls.com.au →

Bureau of Meteorology

Australia's national weather, tide, swell and marine forecasting service.

Starting at Manly Beach

RipCo starts at Manly Beach and is built to scale to other Australian beaches over time. Coverage depends on a beach having a public camera feed to work with. Wherever RipCo reaches, the rule stays the same: swim between the red and yellow flags and follow the lifeguards.
