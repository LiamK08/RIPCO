Features

Five tools that help you read the water.

RipCo brings live conditions, AI detection and clear guidance together in one app. Each tool has the same purpose: help you make a better decision before you get in.

Feature 01

AI Safety Brief

A plain-English summary of what the water is doing at your beach right now. Instead of reading raw numbers, you get a short, direct brief you can understand in seconds, with the reminder to swim between the flags built in.

Feature 02

Live camera with AI rip detection

The live view from your beach camera, with likely rips outlined in red and a confidence percentage. You can see where a rip is and how sure the model is, then check it against the flags before you decide to swim.

Feature 03

Marine Conditions

Wind, swell, tide and water temperature for your beach, gathered into one clear view. The same signals lifeguards weigh up, in front of you before you leave the house, so you know what to expect when you arrive.

Feature 04

Shark Watch

Community-reported hazard sightings, shared with other people at the beach. When someone reports a sighting, others nearby can see it, so the whole beach benefits from more eyes on the water.

Feature 05

Ask RipCo

A conversational assistant for conditions and beach-safety questions. Ask what the surf is doing, what a flag means or how to spot a rip, and get a clear answer that always points you back to the flags and the lifeguards.
